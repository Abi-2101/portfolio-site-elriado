About Us

Siragiri Velan Apparels, under the brand name Elriado, is a trusted name in the clothing industry. Established in 2019, we have rapidly grown into one of the most reputed manufacturers of high-quality knitted garments.

What we make

Premium knitted garments for brands, corporates and schools. From design-to-delivery, we handle bulk production with strict quality checks.

Capacity & Promise

Manufacturing capacity: 50,000 pieces per month. Annual delivery capacity exceeding 5 lakh orders with customization for corporate needs.

Our History

Founded by Mr. Subhash Murugesan in 2019, Siragiri Velan Apparels is built on a strong foundation of passion and commitment. Mr. Subhash began his journey in the textile industry in 2016, and through continuous effort, hard work, and reliability, the company has achieved a remarkable manufacturing capacity of 50,000 pieces per month.
